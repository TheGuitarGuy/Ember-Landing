Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Kennion Gubler ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Ember Fitness mobile application ("App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

Account information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and the display name you choose to provide. This information is used to authenticate you, personalize your experience, and sync your data across devices. We also store account metadata such as your account creation date and last login time.

Workout and fitness data

The App stores your workout history, completed exercises, streaks, scheduled workout days, and monthly activity summaries in our cloud database (Google Firebase). This data is used to power in-app features such as progress tracking, streak calculations, and group activity sharing.

Health data (Apple HealthKit)

With your permission, the App reads the following data from Apple HealthKit:

  • Workout sessions — to import your outdoor runs, walks, and hikes completed in other apps or on Apple Watch
  • Heart rate — to display live heart rate data streamed from Apple Watch during active workouts
  • Walking and running distance — to display live distance data streamed from Apple Watch during active workouts

The App does not write data back to Apple HealthKit. Health data is used solely to power in-app features and is never sold, shared, or transmitted to third parties.

Camera

The App uses your device's camera to analyze body position and count exercise repetitions in real time using on-device processing. No images or video are ever stored on your device or transmitted to any server.

Location

The App uses your GPS location during run and walk tracking workouts to map your route, calculate distance, and measure pace. Location is active only while a run or walk workout is in progress, including when the app is backgrounded during a session. We do not track your location for any other purpose.

Notification tokens

If you enable push notifications, your device's notification token is stored in our cloud database and used solely to deliver workout reminders and App notices to your device.

Usage data

We automatically collect information about how you use the App — such as features visited, workouts completed, and session duration — to understand usage patterns and improve the App. This data is collected through Google Firebase Analytics.

Purchase information

In-app purchases (such as Remove Ads) are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment details. We receive only a confirmation that a purchase was completed in order to unlock the relevant features.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the App and its features
  • Sync your workout history, streaks, and group activity across devices
  • Display relevant advertising (see Section 3)
  • Send push notifications for workout reminders and App notices
  • Respond to your support requests

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

Service Purpose Privacy Policy
Google Firebase Authentication, database, analytics firebase.google.com/support/privacy

AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and device information to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. You can limit ad tracking in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these circumstances:

  • Service providers — third-party vendors (listed above) who process data on our behalf and are contractually required to protect it
  • Legal compliance — when required by law, subpoena, or to protect the rights and safety of users or the public
  • Business transfer — in the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction, with notice provided

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and fitness data for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period after deletion. Analytics data is retained for up to 24 months. To request deletion of your account and all associated data, contact us at tryemberfitness@gmail.com and we will respond within 30 days. You may also delete your account directly within the App under Settings → Account → Delete Account.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Choices

  • Account deletion — Delete your account in-app under Settings → Account → Delete Account, or contact tryemberfitness@gmail.com
  • HealthKit — Revoke HealthKit access at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health
  • Location — Revoke location access at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services
  • Camera — Revoke camera access at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Camera
  • Notifications — Disable notifications in the App or in iOS Settings → Notifications → Ember Fitness
  • Ad tracking — Limit ad personalization in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
  • Uninstalling the App stops all data collection

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encrypted data transmission and access controls on our cloud database. However, no system is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
