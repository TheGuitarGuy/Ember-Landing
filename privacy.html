Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Kennion Gubler ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Ember Fitness mobile application ("App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

Account information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and the display name or profile information you choose to provide. This is used to authenticate you, personalize your experience, and sync your data across devices.

Health and fitness data

With your permission, the App reads from and writes to Apple HealthKit. This includes workout sessions, activity data, and related fitness metrics. Health data is used solely to power in-app features such as workout tracking and progress history. We do not sell, share, or transmit your Health data to third parties.

Camera

The App uses your device's camera to analyze body position and count exercise repetitions in real time. All camera processing happens entirely on your device — no images or video are stored or transmitted to any server.

Location

The App collects your device's GPS location during run tracking workouts. Location data is used to map your route, calculate distance, and measure pace. Location is only active when you start a run. We do not continuously track your location in the background for any other purpose.

Usage data

We automatically collect information about how you use the App — such as features visited, workouts completed, and session duration — to understand usage patterns and improve the App over time. This data is collected in aggregated, anonymized form through Google Firebase Analytics.

Purchase information

In-app purchases (such as Remove Ads) are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment details. We receive only a confirmation that a purchase was completed in order to unlock the relevant features.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the App and its features
  • Sync your workout history, streaks, and group activity across devices
  • Display relevant advertising (see Section 3)
  • Respond to your questions and support requests
  • Send important notices about the App (not marketing, unless you opt in)

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with its own privacy policy:

Service Purpose Privacy Policy
Google Firebase Authentication, cloud data storage, analytics firebase.google.com/support/privacy

AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and device information to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. You can limit ad tracking through your iOS device settings under Privacy & Security → Tracking.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these circumstances:

  • Service providers — third-party vendors (listed above) who process data on our behalf and are contractually required to protect it
  • Legal compliance — when required by law, subpoena, or to protect the rights and safety of users or the public
  • Business transfer — in the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction, with notice provided

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and fitness data for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period after deletion. Automatically collected analytics data is retained for up to 24 months. To request deletion of your account and associated data, contact us at tryemberfitness@gmail.com and we will respond within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Choices

  • Account deletion — Contact tryemberfitness@gmail.com to delete your account and data
  • HealthKit — You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health
  • Location — You can revoke location access at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services
  • Camera — You can revoke camera access at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Camera
  • Ad tracking — Limit ad personalization in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
  • Uninstalling the App stops all data collection

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encrypted data transmission and access controls. However, no system is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
